Analogue to CAN Converter

Developed an analogue to CAN converter for the 2023 Formula SAE car enhancing modularity and real-time sensor data conversion.

  • Altium
  • LTspice
  • C++/C
  • STM32
  • CAN Bus
01

What

Electronics Device for Race Cars

Built a custom electronic device that converts analog sensor signals (like throttle position, brake pressure) into digital CAN bus messages for the Formula SAE racing car's computer systems to allowing for modularity and uniform data exchange.

  • Bridge Sensors

    Convert analogue sensor signals to CAN bus digital format

  • Real-time Performance

    Real time sensor data conversion

  • FSAE Compliance

    Must meet FSAE rules and regulations for design

  • CAN Bus Integration

    Integration with vehicle control network
